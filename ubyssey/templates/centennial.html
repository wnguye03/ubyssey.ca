Since The Ubyssey’s founding 100 years ago, the journalism industry has gone through many changes, and the paper has always managed to keep up with them. UBC has grown and we have grown along with it. But a little help would go a long way — we’re trying to innovate, not just keep our heads above water. As we continue to tell the stories that matter most to students and the wider community, we want to be able to invest in the equipment we need to produce podcasts, shoot video, and put together the type of online journalism that students in 2018 expect of their campus newspaper.

Please consider helping us continue to be Canada’s best student newspaper as we move into our next century, and thank you so much for your generosity.
